How Life GPA Works

  • Create an account. Add your positive habits -- pick from suggested goals and/or customize your own.
  • Each day, select yes for each goal you accomplished. Select no if you didn't do it; try again tomorrow.
  • See your progress, tracked as a percentage for each goal. Also, note your overall average percentage of success -- your Life GPA!

Start making progress today! Life GPA makes it easy to make and practice good habits every day. This mobile-friendly tool is handy and ready to help you keep on track and stay motivated.
