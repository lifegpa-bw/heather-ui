How LifeGPA Works

  1. 1. Create an account. Add your positive habits -- pick from suggested goals and/or customize your own.
  2. 2. Each day, select yes for each goal you accomplished. Select no if you didn't do it; try again tomorrow.
  3. 3. See your progress, tracked as a percentage for each goal. Also, note your overall average percentage of success -- your LifeGPA!
